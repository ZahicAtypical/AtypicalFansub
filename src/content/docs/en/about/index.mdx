# About Atypical Fansub

## Who We Are

We are a team of subtitle enthusiasts who love film and television works. Founded in **2024**, we are dedicated to providing high-quality translated subtitles for Chinese-speaking audiences.

## Our Mission

- **🎯 High-Quality Translation** — Pursuing the standard of faithfulness, expressiveness, and elegance. Every line is carefully crafted.
- **⚡ Fast Release** — Delivering quality subtitles to viewers as soon as possible.
- **🌐 Cultural Bridge** — Connecting audiences across different languages and cultures, letting more people enjoy excellent works.
- **💎 Unified Standards** — Consistent terminology and style guidelines to maintain uniform quality.
